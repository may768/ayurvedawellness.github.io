🧘 What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is a 5,000-year-old system of natural healing that emphasizes balance in bodily systems using diet, lifestyle, and herbal remedies. Key to Ayurveda are the three Doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.
